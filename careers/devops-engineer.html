DevOps Engineer

Jobs Description

Astrum AI Solutions is looking for a talented DevOps Engineer to streamline our software development and deployment pipelines. You will play a critical role in automating workflows, ensuring system reliability, and enabling scalable AI‑driven applications. This position requires expertise in cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, and modern DevOps practices to support our AI and software engineering teams.

Responsibilities:

Key Responsibilities for DevOps Engineer will be as follows

  • Design, implement, and maintain CI/CD pipelines for AI and software projects.
  • Automate infrastructure provisioning and configuration using tools like Terraform, Ansible, or Kubernetes.
  • Monitor system performance, troubleshoot issues, and ensure high availability.
  • Collaborate with developers to integrate machine learning models into production environments.
  • Implement security best practices across cloud and on‑premise systems.
  • Manage containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Optimize deployment workflows for speed, reliability, and scalability.
  • Document processes and provide operational support for development teams.
Requirements:

Before Applying, applicant shall ensure to fulfill, requirement as described below

  • Bachelor’s/Master’s in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in DevOps, cloud engineering, or system administration.
  • Strong knowledge of CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions).
  • Proficiency in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, and microservices architecture.
  • Familiarity with monitoring tools (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack).
  • Solid scripting skills (Python, Bash, or similar).
  • Strong problem‑solving and collaboration abilities.

Seeking highly skilled Machine Learning Expert to design, develop, and deploy advanced AI models that power intelligent applications.
