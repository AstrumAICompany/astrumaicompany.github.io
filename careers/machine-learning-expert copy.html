Machine Learning Expert

Jobs Description

Astrum AI Solutions is seeking a highly skilled Machine Learning Expert to design, develop, and deploy advanced AI models that power intelligent applications. This role involves working on cutting‑edge projects such as agentic AI systems, Retrieval‑Augmented Generation (RAG), intelligent translation, and predictive analytics. You will collaborate with software engineers, data scientists, and product teams to deliver scalable, future‑proof solutions.

Responsibilities:

Key Responsibilities for Machine Learning Expert will be as follows

  • Design and implement machine learning models for diverse business applications.
  • Develop and optimize algorithms for NLP, computer vision, and predictive analytics.
  • Integrate ML models into production systems with robust APIs and pipelines.
  • Research and apply state‑of‑the‑art techniques in agentic AI and RAG.
  • Collaborate with cross‑functional teams to translate business needs into ML solutions.
  • Monitor, evaluate, and retrain models to ensure accuracy and scalability.
  • Document processes, workflows, and model performance for transparency.
Requirements:

Before Applying, applicant shall ensure to fulfill, requirement as described below

  • Master’s or PhD in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, or related field.
  • Strong proficiency in Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit‑learn.
  • Experience with NLP, deep learning, and reinforcement learning.
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, GCP) and MLOps practices.
  • Familiarity with RAG pipelines, intelligent translation, and agentic AI concepts.
  • Excellent problem‑solving, analytical, and communication skills.
  • 3+ years of experience in applied machine learning projects.
